ABOUT

I am a Senior Researcher at Tencent Hunyuan, focusing on post-training for vision-language models and frontier AGI capabilities. My work centers on reinforcement learning, reward modeling, and agentic learning, with a strong emphasis on GUI agents, vision-language reasoning, and real-world task solving. By bridging scalable post-training with deployment-oriented research, I aim to advance the frontier capabilities of generalist AI systems. Previously, I contributed to LLM alignment at Xiaohongshu, AIGC at Kuaishou, and autonomous driving at Tencent YouTu Lab.
